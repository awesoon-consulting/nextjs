# Task: AI Integration, SEO Expansion, and Internal Linking

## Objective

Strengthen Awesoon's website positioning by turning AI from an implied capability into a documented value-add across the existing solutions portfolio.

The goal is not to pivot the company into a generic AI agency. The goal is to show how AI, RAG, and agentic workflows support Awesoon's core operational consulting offers:

- systems audits
- operations infrastructure
- spreadsheet replacement
- system integration
- ERP implementation
- CRM implementation
- API integration
- vendor selection

## Why This Matters

Current messaging strongly explains operational pain and implementation support, but it leaves a gap around how modern AI capabilities fit into those engagements.

Adding that layer improves:

- SEO coverage for AI-adjacent search intent
- internal link density between commercial and editorial pages
- perceived strategic depth for buyers evaluating modernization partners
- conversion pathways from educational content into service pages

## Strategy

### 1. Add AI value to every solution page

Each solution should explain how AI can create additional leverage after foundational systems, data quality, and workflows are stabilized.

Examples:

- RAG assistants for SOPs, policy retrieval, and team onboarding
- agentic workflows for exception triage and repetitive operational tasks
- AI copilots for ERP, CRM, reporting, and account context
- AI monitoring layers for integrations and workflow failures

### 2. Avoid hype-led positioning

AI should be framed as an operational multiplier, not a replacement for process discipline.

Awesoon's angle should remain:

- operator-led
- implementation-first
- governance-aware
- mid-market practical

### 3. Improve internal linking

Every solution page should link to:

- adjacent solutions
- relevant insight articles

Every insight article should link back to:

- one or more relevant service pages
- other supporting insight articles

This improves crawlability, topical clustering, and user flow.

### 4. Expand editorial coverage

Create supporting insight content around:

- AI readiness for operations teams
- where RAG fits in operations
- where agentic workflows make sense

These pieces should support both search intent and sales conversations.

- Added related insights blocks to solution detail pages
- Extended the insights model with `relatedSolutionSlugs`
- Added two new AI-focused insight articles
- Added related solutions and "keep reading" internal links on insight detail pages

## Follow-Up Opportunities

- Add JSON-LD for service pages and articles
- Add dedicated landing pages for AI operations consulting, RAG implementation, and agentic workflow design if these become priority offers
- Expand multilingual translations so new AI content exists in FR and ES
- Review homepage and navigation to decide whether AI deserves explicit surface-level placement
- Add CTA variants tailored to AI-readiness discovery calls

## Success Criteria

- Awesoon remains grounded in operations consulting rather than drifting into vague AI messaging
- AI terminology is connected to concrete operational outcomes
- solution and insight pages reinforce each other through clear internal links
- metadata and on-page copy support broader search visibility
