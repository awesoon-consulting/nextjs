## D000 — North Star (Permanent — never archive)
**Status:** Active

"Does this content feel unmistakably like Awesoon, or could it be any operations consulting website?"

Awesoon is operator-led, implementation-first, and governance-aware — positioned for mid-market companies that need real operational change, not strategy decks. Every piece of copy must reflect that AI and technology serve as operational multipliers built on process discipline, data quality, and systems maturity. Content that drifts into generic AI hype, vague transformation language, or enterprise-consultant posturing fails the D000 test.

## D001 — Sentence case in all headlines
**Status:** Active
**Source:** Ray

All headlines and section titles must use sentence case. Never title case.
Correct: "Why work with us"
Wrong: "Why Work With Us"
